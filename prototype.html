What has the Rock Kai'lani been cooking?

Describing It

The prototype software that I have developed is a rudimentary edition of a program that I plan to further advance. This software tracks eyes detected on a video feed (currently supplied via webcam) using facial recognition, further into eye recognition, and even further into blob recognition to differentiate the pupils from the rest of the eye to create a working visual to demonstrate that I was able to develop a program that can track eye movement and opens the possibility to record data about the paths that the eyes create and can be observed with the eye tracking technology.

What are the Specs?

This prototype is what I have referred to it as very clearly earlier on, it is a rudimentary edition of what I plan to fully implement to conduct experiments for my intended research.

That being said, the project is rather lightweight. Currently I put it together and use Poetry to manage the dependencies, so it's near ready to use "out-of-box" (after you type in a pesky poetry install).

What's the Plan?

After the prototype, I most definitely want to continue pursuit of newer releases of this software and continue to refine it to the utmost perfection that I know it can be in order to demonstrate the level of knowledge I hold in computer science and to be best fit to assist in conducting experimental research.

Next steps absolutely include testing newer features slowly to validate the logic behind their additions to ultimately build the software necessary for my desired outcome of my research and the presentation of such.
