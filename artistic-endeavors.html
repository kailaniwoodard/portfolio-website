Who am I
Artistically?

The development of my creative pursuit.

From a very early age, I felt rather engaged with the arts. Funnily enough, by the time I reached high school, I never took any art classes that would point me in the direction to use what many may call traditional mediums. However, I did find myself on the path of working with more digital mediums by participating in my high school's photography and film clubs. From there, most of my artistic drive developed very heavily.

Currently, I find myself pursuing an art degree alongside my intended computer science degree. This originated in Allegheny's policy of pursuing a minor that is interdisciplinary from one's intended major. From there, I reignited my passion for art and took the leap to pursue an art major along with my computer science major.
