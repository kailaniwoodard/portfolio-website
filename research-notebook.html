Introduction

Further information about myself and research interests past the autobiography.

In regards to what I am interesting in researching, it heavily correlates to what I am currently studying at Allegheny, of which being degrees in Art, Science and Innovation and also Computer Science.

Computer Science can often be viewed as a non-artistic path of study, however thinking that the combination of artistic methods alongside computer science is an oddity or something that isn't achievable is simply not a valid way of thinking.

Personally I find myself enjoying all of the combinations of computer science and art, however specific interests of study that I am personally passionate about in this cross-section are certainly involved with visualization of data and using machine learning in art creation.

Now, you may ask: "How do you personally plan to implement such methods of combining these disciplines and create something you would like to research?" Well that's certainoly a question I find myself stuck on from time to time. However, in my current pursuits, I would absolutely love to continue the pursuit of one of my personal projects, Curatrix for Spotify, and adapt it to this class. I feel that in doing this, it satisfies the personal requirements that I have laid out for myself in informal terms; continuing further development to this application involves non-invasive data analysis, manipulates that data, and creates a product for the user to view and enjoy. This is a very base-level of thinking in regards to combining both art and computer science, however, in further research into the juxtaposition of the two, involvement of further automation or "beautiful data visualization" could certainly set me up in the correct direction to create my senior thesis or adapt this project into such.
