Who am I
Computationally?

The journey that led me to where I am today.

From an early age, I found myself drawn to the technical side of things. It started with the utmost basic forms by creating simple programs in Scratch with block coding, then eventually graduating to writing code and creating scenes in Alice, then to writing basic Minecraft mods, which evolved into pursuing further education into computer science through the coursework available to me during high school, and ultimately brought me to where I am today.

At this point in my life, I would like to consider myself a computer scientist of sorts. I have been pursuing my undergraduate degree in Computer Science at Allegheny College since Fall 2019 and intend on obtaining this degree by May 2023.
