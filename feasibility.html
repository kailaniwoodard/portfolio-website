How Feasible
is it Really?

Discussing if I'm really reaching for the stars.

What are the Qualifiers for Feasibility?

When exploring the feasibility of research, it is certainly important to consider each of the fine details to ensure that the intended pursuit of research is plain-out achievable to begin with.

Here, I'm just going to be discussing some points briefly to validate the feasibility of my proposed research project.

Hardware

I'm anticipating fairly reasonable requirements with hardware when considering the initial research I want to perform. Putting it simply, I would need a machine with the minimum system requirements of:

1080p @ 30 FPS or 720p @ 60 FPS webcam

Windows 10 or macOS X

4-core Intel processor, 3GHz or faster, 5th gen or later.

DirectX 12 graphics card (with updated drivers, of course)

This requirement is already met on the level that is necessary for initial conduct of research, however for the artistic presentation, there is still not a certain plan for the exact hardware required, which is pending upon proposal approval and accessibility to funding.

Tools

I plan to write my eye tracking software with Python, as it is fitting to use the language for this type of purpose, especially considering the libraries available in Python.

Some tools and resources that I anticipate using in my research are:

Pytorch

OpenCV

These have their own reasons for being helpful, but both generally contribute to helping use existing resources to develop a program that does exactly as I want without having to reinvent the wheel.

Data and Anticipated Results

In regards to data for the project, it will need generated and I am operating under the assumption that this data will be generated by a participation study that I will have to conduct. I plan to store my findings in a local database, likely using SQLite or MySQL.

The kind of results I expect to yield from this experiment would ultimately aide me in supporting my proposed hypothesis about the male gaze, but generally needs to be able to collect the values that a pupil will be positioned in the webcam feed or video and the path that is created by putting these values together.

The analysis of the data would be conducted heavily through comparison and finding common attributes per participant in the study, then consider the concepts attached to the "male gaze" theory to further come to a conclusion and also use these findings in my artistic presentation.

Do I Feel Like I Can Do It?

I am incredibly confident in my intellectual and general computational ability to assist in the completion of this project, I feel that with the time given and the automation that can be performed with data analysis based on what I collect, a heavy amount of the work will rely on further research and drawing connections to support my hypothesis.
